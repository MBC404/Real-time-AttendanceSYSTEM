Connections


RFID RC522 Pin	Arduino UNO Pin	Description
VCC(3.3v) 	3.3V	Power supply (⚠️ Use 3.3V)
GND	GND	Ground connection
SDA (SS)	Pin 10	Serial Data (SPI Chip Select)
SCK	Pin 13	Serial Clock (SPI Clock)
MOSI	Pin 11	Master Out Slave In (SPI Data)
MISO	Pin 12	Master In Slave Out (SPI Data)
RST	Pin 9	Reset Pin
